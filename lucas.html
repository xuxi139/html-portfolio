Lucas (윤덕 hee)

About Lucas

Lucas (윤덕 hee) is a singer, rapper, and dancer from SuperM, a project group formed by SM Entertainment. He was born on January 25, 1999, in Seoul, South Korea, and later moved to Hong Kong with his family. Lucas was discovered by SM Entertainment through their global auditions in Hong Kong in 2015 and joined the company as a trainee. In 2018, he debuted as a member of NCT and WayV, and later that year, he was introduced as a member of SuperM. Lucas is known for his powerful and versatile vocals, as well as his charismatic stage presence and dance skills.
